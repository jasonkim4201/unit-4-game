Crystals Collector

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There will be four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

To win you will need to match your total score with the randomly generated number. If you go above the randomly generated number, you lose.

The value of each crystal of hidden until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
